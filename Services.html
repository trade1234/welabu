CHICKPEAS

Chickpeas, or garbanzo beans, are a protein-rich legume packed with fiber, folate, and iron. They're versatile, used in dishes like hummus, salads, and stews. Chickpeas also contribute to sustainable farming by improving soil health through nitrogen fixation.
